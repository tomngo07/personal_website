Minecraft is a sandbox game developed by a company called Mojang. I love to play their "survival" game mode and let my creativity take me away

CS:GO

CounterStrike Global Offensive is also one of my favorite games. It is a first person shooter game where you and your teammates have to eliminate the other team before they eliminate you. You can also win by planting the bomb or saving the hostage, this all varies depending on the map. Ever since CSGO became free, a lot of coders have managed to create cheats for the game, so now most of the community are cheaters, including myself. There are custom games created by players where only cheaters come and play. This is the type of server that I like to play on.

FIFA MOBILE

FIFA Mobile is the official mobile version of the realistic soccer game where there are a variety of game modes to choose from. The most popular (and only game mode) you can play when on a mobile device is ultimate team. This is where you start from nothing and work your way up by collecting coins and obtaining players. In ultimate team there are multiple ways to collect coins, you can win online matches, do objectives, buy and sell players, etc.
